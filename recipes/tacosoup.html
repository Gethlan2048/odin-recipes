Taco Soup

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • onion
  • Rotel Tomatoes
  • Cream Cheese
  • beef broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and onions together
  2. In a large sauce pan add ground beef and softened cream cheese on medium heat.
  3. Stir together.
  4. Add salt and pepper taste.
  5. Served with garnished cheese.

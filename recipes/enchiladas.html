Enchiladas

Growing up in McAllen, TX I learned to love the flavor of the chilli sauce that was used for the Tex-Mex enchilada plates.

Today I will show you how to make this amazing Texas dish with minimal effort, so that you and your family will enjoy a wonderful dinner tonight.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Chilli Gravy
  • 6 corn tortillas
  • 12 Kraft singles
  • one medium onion

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450
  2. Pour 1/2 cup gravy in pan
  3. Put two slices of cheese in each tortilla
  4. Roll up tortillas and pour on remaining gravy
  5. Sprinkle on cheese
  6. Bake for 10 minutes, or until it bubbles
  7. Serve the enchiladas

An excellent lasagna recipe. All credit goes to brenda. I have not tried it yet; however, she has rave reviews on recipe

How to make it will be described below. It is simple to make and for lasagna it is relatively quick. I hope you enjoy this recipe that I had nothing to do with.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • .5 pound of shredded cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Heat oven to 350. Brown meat.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, Cheddar and ricotta; stir well. In 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
